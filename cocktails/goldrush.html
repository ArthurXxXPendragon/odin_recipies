Gold Rush Cocktail

Description:

The gold rush cocktail is a beautiful blend of bourbon, honey, and lemon that is perfect for an after dinner accent or a refreshing poolside beverage on a hot day.

The simple ingredents and easy to make nature of the drink make it a cocktail that doesn't take a bartender to perfect but will impress anyone who drinks it.

Ingredients:

  • 0.5-1oz Honey
  • 2oz Bourbon
  • 0.75oz Lemon - Freshly Squeezed

Directions:

  1. Mix 0.5-1oz honey and 0.5oz water to make 1-1.5oz of honey syrup depending on desired sweetness.
  2. Add 0.75oz honey syrup, 0.75oz of freshly squeezed lemon, and 2oz of bourbon to a shaker with ice and shake until chilled.
  3. Use strainer to strain over ice, and garnish with a lemon twist if desired.
